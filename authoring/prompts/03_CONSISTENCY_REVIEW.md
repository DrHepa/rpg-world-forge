# Session: consistency and reachability review

Receive already-authored facts, actors, arcs, dialogue, scenes, and quests. Do
not rewrite canon automatically.

Find:

- Broken references or duplicate IDs.
- Actors using information outside their knowledge boundaries.
- Scenes made impossible by schedule, location, or quest state.
- Softlocks, circular rewards, and choices without consequences.
- Contradictions between a personal campaign and global state.
- Dominant mechanics that invalidate other paths.
- Different content producing the same result unintentionally.

Return evidence, impact, a minimal reproduction path, and two possible fixes
for every finding. The lead agent or human owner makes the final decision.
