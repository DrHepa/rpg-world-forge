# Session: world foundation

## Required human inputs

- Premise and central player fantasy.
- Tone, age rating, and excluded themes.
- Spatial and temporal scale.
- Gameplay pillars and mechanics that must interact.
- Physical, social, economic, magical, or technological laws.
- Language, ID conventions, and canonical sources.

## Expected result

1. Atomic canonical facts with stable IDs.
2. Places and factions with needs, resources, and conflicts.
3. A system matrix showing what changes what and through which events.
4. Contradiction risks and questions requiring a material decision.
5. A vertical-slice proposal exercising at least three connected systems.

Do not generate hundreds of pages of inert lore. Prioritize facts that create
observable choices, consequences, or mechanics.
