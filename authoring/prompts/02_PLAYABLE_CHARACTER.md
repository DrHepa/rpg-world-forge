# Session: playable actor and personal campaign

Design the actor as data independent from the engine and base world.

## Required content

- Identity, internal need, external desire, and central contradiction.
- Competencies, weaknesses, costs, and unique gameplay verbs.
- Facts known, suspected, protected, or impossible to know.
- Directed relationships and how they may change.
- A personal multi-act arc with failure, recovery, and closure states.
- Crossings with the world conflict without duplicating the main quest.
- Conditions, events, and effects required to implement it without runtime prose
  generation.

Verify that the campaign remains understandable whether played before or after
the other actors' campaigns.
